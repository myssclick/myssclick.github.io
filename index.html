Welcome To our blog !

During our semester abroad on Erasmus, we’ll have the chance to dive deeper into the human, cultural, economic, and geo-political aspects of our host city and country. This experience will offer us a unique perspective and help us better understand the local way of life.

100% Honnest

We promise to be honest about our experiences and share our true opinions throughout this trip. Whether it's the highs or the challenges, we'll give you an authentic look at what life as an Erasmus student in Budapest really feels like.

Real Insights, Real Tips

We also promise to keep our content engaging and informative, sharing helpful tips, local insights, and personal stories. From uncovering hidden gems in the city to navigating cultural differences, we’ll make sure you have valuable takeaways.
